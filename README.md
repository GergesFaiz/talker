# talker

A fast, reliable messaging app that keeps you connected with friends and family anytime, anywhere. Send text messages, photos, videos, and files instantly with secure end‑to‑end encryption. Enjoy high‑quality voice and video calls, and share your daily moments through short status updates. With a simple, user‑friendly interface, staying in touch has never been easier.
